Pair Programming Session

Peer pairing is a way to approach problem and find a solution wit h a partner.

Driver vs Navigator

You have two different people working together. First, you have the driver who writes all of the code. Secondly, you have a navigator who guides his or her partner. If you were driving a car, a driver would be on the gas pedal while the navigator would be steering.

Why You Think Pair-Programming is Important to DBC

In DBC, there is a wide range of skills and expertise. There are some experienced programmers and some inexperienced programmers. Peer programming gives both people opportunities to learn. The experienced programmer can learn how to communicate their thought process while the inexperienced programmer can better their technical skills. In addition, peer programming teaches you how to work on a team.
